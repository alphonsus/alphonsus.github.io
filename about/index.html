About

Hello! I am Alfonz Montelibano a.k.a. Alphonsus—a full-stack web developer by day, moonlighting as an indie game developer at Supernaught by night. This website will be my technical dev blog of sorts where I talk about web dev, and game dev, or just software development in general.

I have been doing game jams along with a friend, Dave, who does all the art work, since 2013. Some cool games I worked on are Slugcaster, Necromorph, Pong Knights, Hammerfall, and JUMPR. Check out the complete list here!

I made a page about the tools I use for making games, if you’re into that. Also I made LDJAM Bot, a Twitter bot that tweets random Ludum Dare games.

 

Anything else?

When I’m not writing software, I travel with my girlfriend, take lots of photos, or make some music.
