About Blog Projects

Hello! I am Alfonz Montelibano a.k.a. Alphonsus, indie game developer at Supernaught.

I started making games when I was in college. I used Slick2D back then, then later moved to Unity and very recently, HaxeFlixel. I have been doing game jams along with a friend, Dave, who does all the art work, since 2013.

Some cool games I worked on are Necromorph, Pong Knights, Hammerfall, and JUMPR. Check out the complete list here!

I made a page about the tools I use for making games, if you’re into that. Also I made LDJAM Bot, a Twitter bot made in Python that tweets random Ludum Dare games.

Follow me on Twitter @alphnsus for bite-sized updates on projects I’m currently working on. Also, you can reach me at m.alfonz@gmail.com.
